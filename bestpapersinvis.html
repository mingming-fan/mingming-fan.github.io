Best Paper Awards in Visualization

Background: When I recently started my career in the Computational Media and Arts (CMA) Thrust at HKUST. I realized that our students had incredibly diverse academic backgrounds and needed some example papers to get started in the field of Visualization. While the papers that won Best Paper Awards are not necessarily ones that have most significant impacts to the field and our societies, they nevertheless provide some examples, in terms of writing, ways of executing research, or research topics, for new comers to study and also for insiders to reflect upon. As a result, I curate the following list of recently published papers that won Best Paper Awards in the field of Visualization.

If you are interested in Best Paper Awards in the field of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), please check out the following list that I curate: Best Paper Awards in the Human-Computer Interaction

If you are interested in Best Paper Awards in the whole field of Computer Science (CS), Jeff Huang maintains an awesome list.
